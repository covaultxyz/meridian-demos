What Is a Terminal?

A Meridian Terminal is a deployed AI operating system for a business. It's not a chatbot, not a CRM, not a single tool — it's the entire intelligent layer that sits on top of your operations.

**The submarine analogy**: A terminal is a fully crewed submarine. It comes with:

• A **captain** (the orchestrating daemon) that makes decisions

• A **crew** (specialized agents) at their stations, ready to work

• **Protocols** that govern how the crew operates

• A **brain** (MIG knowledge graph) that remembers everything

• **Tools** (integrations, dashboards, voice, research) that the crew uses

• **Redundancy** (backup systems, A/B failover)

You don't get a kit of parts. You get a functioning vehicle.
