OF1.4 — Performance Service Fee Methodology

**Baseline Profit for Equinavia:** Average monthly gross profit attributable to Equinavia during the three (3) calendar months immediately preceding the Order Form Effective Date, as reported by the Fort Knox invoicing system export. Rankona will deliver the Baseline Profit export within five (5) business days of the Order Form Effective Date, with supporting detail sufficient for Provider to verify the calculation.

**Gross profit definition:** For purposes of this Order Form, "gross agency profit attributable to Equinavia" means (revenue received from Equinavia under all contracted fee and commission arrangements) minus (direct costs attributable to serving Equinavia, including allocated staff time at documented loaded cost, tool costs directly attributable to Equinavia work, and third-party contractor fees specifically billed for Equinavia). It excludes Rankona's general overhead not attributable to a specific End Client.

**Measurement period:** Calendar month.

**Calculation:** For each calendar month beginning in Month 4, Increased Profitability = max(0, current-month Equinavia gross profit − Baseline Profit).

**Dispute resolution:** If the Parties cannot agree on Increased Profitability for any month, the matter is escalated to an independent accountant mutually selected; costs shared equally. Disputed amounts accrue but do not trigger late-payment interest until resolved.

**Baseline refresh:** If the End Client business fundamentally changes (e.g., category expansion, acquisition, loss of 25%+ of SKU base for reasons unrelated to Provider's Services), either Party may request a baseline refresh; refresh methodology agreed in good faith.

**Cap / floor:** None. Performance Service Fee is uncapped on upside. No floor — if Increased Profitability is zero or negative for any month, no Performance Service Fee is due for that month; the fixed monthly service fee remains due.
