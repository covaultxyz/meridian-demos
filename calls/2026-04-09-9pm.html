Decisions Made

Key Discoveries

  • Their existing daemon orchestration is 7900 lines of code, prompting a plan to rebuild a leaner, self-contained customer bot.
  • Neo4j is better suited for Python scripts for heavy lifting rather than direct, token-heavy LLM interaction for reasoning.
