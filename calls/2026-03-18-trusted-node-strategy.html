Trusted Node Strategy — Go-to-Market Framework

**Origin**: March 18 call — Bitler articulated this as the primary sales motion

**Core principle**: "Building at the speed of trust" (Ely Beckman)

---

The Model

Traditional sales: Company → Marketing → Cold leads → Nurture → Close → Client

**Trusted Node sales**: Company → Trusted Node → Warm referral → Demonstrate value → Close → Client → Trusted Node sends more clients

A **Trusted Node** is someone who:

1. Has an established audience of business owners

2. Has earned trust through years of service (not marketing)

3. Benefits when their clients benefit (referral fees, reputation, retention)

4. Can credibly say "you should talk to these guys"

---

Identified Trusted Node Types

CPAs / Accountants

• **Why they're nodes**: Every business has one. They see the financials. They're trusted advisors.

• **Access**: 500-1,000 clients per large firm

• **What motivates them**: Making their clients more profitable (keeps clients), efficiency gains for their own firm, advisory revenue (shift from compliance to planning)

• **Our pitch to them**: "We make your clients more profitable. We make your firm more efficient. You look like a hero for the introduction."

• **Active lead**: Luis Lamba (Bitler's CPA in San Juan)

Business Brokers

• **Why they're nodes**: They have 50-100+ businesses looking to sell at any time

• **Access**: Direct to business owners who are motivated to spend money (preparing for the biggest transaction of their life)

• **What motivates them**: Higher sale prices = higher commissions. Faster sales = more deal flow.

• **Our pitch to them**: "We make your listings worth more. Companies sell faster when they're documented and systematized."

• **Active lead**: Nate (Bitler's friend)

Attorneys (Business / M&A / Tax)

• **Why they're nodes**: Handle transactions, disputes, structuring. See the inside of every business.

• **Access**: 100-500 clients per practice

• **What motivates them**: Better-prepared clients = smoother transactions = less liability = more referrals

• **Our pitch to them**: "Your clients will come to closings with cleaner data rooms and documented operations. Due diligence goes from 3 months to 4 weeks."

• **Active lead**: None yet — develop through CPA and broker relationships

Industry Community Leaders

• **Why they're nodes**: Built audiences of buyers through content, events, communities (Skool, mastermind groups)

• **Access**: Hundreds to thousands of followers who trust their recommendations

• **What motivates them**: Commission, content (demo our product as content), community value (bringing real solutions)

• **Our pitch to them**: "We build AI operating systems your community members actually need. You get a revenue share for every client you introduce."

• **Active lead**: Bitler's Amazon seller community (400-500 sellers)

Cost Aggregation / Efficiency Consultants

• **Why they're nodes**: Already going into businesses to find savings (LED upgrades, tax credits, energy optimization)

• **Access**: Walk through the same doors we want to walk through

• **What motivates them**: Adding AI efficiency to their service offering, cross-sell revenue

• **Our pitch to them**: "We're the digital version of what you do physically. Your client gets cost savings from you AND operational intelligence from us."

• **Active lead**: Alan (Alex's contact in Ohio, does cost aggregation through CPAs)

What Each Node Gets

For CPAs

• Free assessment of their own firm's efficiency gaps

• Terminal pilot for their own operations (Starter tier, 30 days)

• 15% revenue share on all referred clients

• Co-branded "AI Readiness Assessment" they can offer to clients as a value-add

For Business Brokers

• Free "business value assessment" tool they can use with listings

• Performance-based fee structure (zero risk to the broker's client)

• Higher commissions from higher sale prices

• Marketing asset: "We don't just list your business. We make it worth more."

For Community Leaders

• Revenue share on all community-sourced clients

• Demo/content opportunities (show the product to their audience)

• Early access to new features

• Co-hosted workshops/webinars

---

Key Principles

1. **Always demonstrate on THEIR business first** — Don't ask them to trust us with their clients until they've seen us work on their own operations

2. **The 48-hour rule** — After every discovery meeting, deliver something impressive within 48 hours. The speed is the differentiator.

3. **Performance-based removes all friction** — If we're willing to bet on our results, the node is confident recommending us

4. **Never sell past the node** — If the CPA introduces us, the CPA stays involved. Don't go around them.

5. **Every client is a future node** — Satisfied clients become recommenders. Design every engagement to create a shareable success story.
