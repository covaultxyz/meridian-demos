---

What It Is

A structured daily check-in protocol delivered through Telegram that forces clarity on priorities, surfaces blind spots, and creates an audit trail of decisions and progress over time.

**Not a to-do list.** Not a time tracker. It's a cognitive calibration tool — seven questions that approach the same core issue from different angles until the user has unshakable clarity on what matters most.

---

How Ely Uses It

The Protocol

• **Morning check-in**: 7 questions, takes 15-20 minutes to answer thoroughly

• **Afternoon check-in** (optional): Quick recalibration

• **Evening check-in** (optional): Day-end reflection, tomorrow's setup

• **Minimum viable**: One per day (morning OR evening) is enough

The 7 Morning Questions (Ely's Version)

1. What is the single most important thing you need to accomplish today?

2. What would make today a failure if it didn't happen?

3. What did you learn yesterday that changes today's priority?

4. What are you avoiding that you know matters?

5. If you could only work 2 hours today, what would you do?

6. What's the gap between what you're planning to do and what you should be doing?

7. Given everything you know right now — what's the one thing?

**The effect**: "By the time you do that, you're left with an unshakable, no shadow of a doubt — what is the most important priority for you to do? And once you have that unshakable clarity, you'd be an idiot to not just do that first thing."

What It Catches

• **Blind spots**: Questions 3, 4, and 6 specifically target things you're missing or avoiding

• **Drift**: If your daily actions don't align with weekly/monthly goals, the check-in surfaces that

• **Priority confusion**: When you have 15 things to do, the 7 questions force you to one

• **Pattern recognition**: Over weeks of check-ins, the AI spots recurring themes ("You keep avoiding the sales calls. Why?")

Productized Version — For Client Terminals

Individual Check-In (Employee/Operator)

**Delivered via**: Telegram (morning message, user responds)

**Time**: 10-15 minutes

**Frequency**: Daily (configurable)

**Protocol**:

1. AI sends personalized questions based on user's role, active projects, and recent check-in history

2. User responds in natural language

3. AI synthesizes responses into:

• Today's #1 priority (confirmed by user through the question cascade)

• Flagged items (things the user is avoiding, recurring delays, misaligned tasks)

• Yesterday's progress vs plan (auto-compared)

4. Summary stored in MIG for continuity

5. Manager/owner can view team check-in summaries (opt-in, transparent)

Team Check-In (Manager/Owner View)

**Delivered via**: Dashboard + Telegram digest

**Frequency**: Daily summary (end of day) or real-time

**What the manager sees**:

• Each team member's #1 priority for the day

• Items that have been pushed off 3+ days in a row (stalled work)

• Alignment score: are individual priorities aligned with team/company priorities?

• Blind spot alerts: "Sarah has mentioned customer complaints 3 times this week but no action item has been created"

• Progress trends: who's consistently delivering, who's consistently reprioritizing

Executive Check-In (CEO/Owner)

**Delivered via**: Weekly synthesis (Telegram or email)

**Frequency**: Weekly

**What the owner sees**:

• Aggregate team alignment with company goals

• Top 3 organizational blind spots (surfaced from individual check-ins)

• Recurring blockers across the team

• Decision queue: items that need owner input (surfaced from team check-ins)

• Week-over-week progress on key initiatives
