What This Is

Lex is MERIDIAN's legal intelligence terminal, co-located on big-white with the caselaw knowledge graph. It serves two simultaneous missions:

Mission A — MERIDIAN Internal Legal Layer

Every agreement MERIDIAN signs, every contract MERIDIAN reviews, every compliance question we answer — runs through Lex. Martin on Lex drafts our MSAs, reviews client contracts before we sign, flags regulatory exposure, and manages DocuSeal workflows. This is our general counsel function.

Mission B — Law Firm KaaS Product

Law firms pay to access the caselaw intelligence layer: 11.08M federal cases with BM25 + vector search, PageRank precedent scoring, citation graph traversal, circuit split detection, and AI-assisted contract analysis. Every contract MERIDIAN reviews enriches the same infrastructure KaaS customers use. Internal use IS product development.

---

Why big-white (Not a VPS)

big-white is already running a production-grade caselaw Neo4j:

• **11.08M Case nodes** (CourtListener + LePaRD, deduped)

• **3.66M Party nodes** (plaintiff/defendant extracted)

• **960K Judge nodes** (from people-db)

• **6.37M CITES edges** (LePaRD forward/backward citations with context snippets)

• **26 indexes ONLINE**: BM25 full-text, 768d HNSW cosine, PageRank scores, case_id unique

• **GTX 1070 Ti**: local embeddings — legal documents never leave the machine for embedding

• **64GB RAM, 3.1TB storage available**: headroom for CAP (6.7M more cases) + document corpus

Line 1: Case Intelligence

**Station 1A — Case Search & Recall** (`caselaw_search`)

• BM25 full-text + PageRank boosting across 11M+ opinions

• Jurisdiction-aware (9th Circuit, SCOTUS, D.C., etc.)

• Returns: case_id, citation, court, year, pagerank, summary snippet

**Station 1B — Precedent Analysis** (`case_precedent_chain`)

• Backward: foundational authorities this case relied on

• Forward: who cited this case? (precedential influence map)

• Depth 1-3 hops, PageRank-ranked

**Station 1C — Contradiction & Circuit Split Detection** (`caselaw_circuit_splits`)

• Same legal issue → different holdings across circuits

• Heuristic (BM25-based grouping by jurisdiction)

• Flags cases for targeted legal analysis

---

Line 2: Document Intelligence

**Station 2A — Contract Drafting** (Martin, template library)

• Template types: MSA, EPA, NDA, SOW, IP assignment, DPA, operating agreement

• Output: clean draft + clause-by-clause risk memo

**Station 2B — Contract Review & Redline** (Martin)
