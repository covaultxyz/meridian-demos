Tier 1: Quick Wins

Execute in hours. Free proof of competence.

Fixes that can be done during or immediately after the meeting. Each one is objectively wrong and trivially fixable.

1

Add Meta Descriptions to All 10 Pages

2 hours

Current State

Zero meta descriptions on any page. Google generates random snippets from page body.

The Fix

Write optimized meta descriptions for all 10 pages. Install Yoast or RankMath for ongoing management.

Leverage Quote

"Right now when someone Googles your company name, Google decides what to show. We can fix that in an afternoon."

2

Add Schema.org Structured Data

3 hours

Current State

No JSON-LD structured data. No rich snippets in Google results.

The Fix

Add Organization schema (with Platinum badge), Service schema, FAQ schema on /why-amazon/.

Leverage Quote

"You just got Amazon's Tier 1 Platinum status. But Google doesn't know that because your site doesn't tell it."

3

Fix the Cookie Policy

15 min

Current State

References helenethituson.com and superchargeyourblog.com. Template never customized.

The Fix

Replace all third-party domain references with rankonamazon.com. Update GDPR data controller info.

Leverage Quote

"Your cookie policy currently credits someone else's website. 15-minute fix, but it matters for GDPR."

4

Fix the eGuide Download Button

30 min

Current State

"8 Success Factors" eGuide button is a span with no href. Does nothing when clicked.

The Fix

Convert to anchor tag with PDF link. Gate behind email capture for nurture sequence.

Leverage Quote

"Your lead magnet has a broken download button. Every person who tried to download it got nothing. 30-minute fix."

5

Add Amazon Platinum Partner Badge

1 hour

Current State

Tier 1 Platinum Partner (Feb 2025) appears nowhere on the website. Zero trust badges displayed.

The Fix

Add badge to homepage hero, about page, footer. Include in Schema.org Organization markup.

Leverage Quote

"You have Amazon's highest agency credential in the EU and it's not on your website. That badge sells better than any copy."

6

Update Copyright & Security Hardening

45 min

Current State

Copyright says 2023. XML-RPC enabled. REST API leaks usernames. Pingback enabled.

The Fix

Update copyright to 2026. Disable XML-RPC, restrict REST API, disable pingback.

Leverage Quote

"Small thing — footer still says 2023. Bigger thing — your admin accounts are publicly visible. Both easy fixes."

The "85 vs 100" Inconsistency

/why-us/ page

"85+ years of successful selling on Amazon"

/about/ page

"100+ years of Amazon selling experience"

Why it matters: Proves no one reviews the site content. Both numbers are live and contradict each other.

How to use: Do NOT raise unless conversation naturally goes to site quality. Use as a gentle example, never a gotcha.

The reframe: "Small things like this are exactly what we catch and fix. Your team is focused on client work — as they should be."
