Intelligence Summary

1

The site is a migration artifact, not a built website

Everything traces back to January 2023 migration from amanordic.com. GUIDs, emails, cookie policies still reference the old domain.

2

Helene Thituson built the site and still has admin access

User ID 1 is her email. Cookie policy references her other sites. External contractor with admin access is a security concern.

3

Zero content marketing despite 44 podcast episodes

25+ hours of expert content sitting on Buzzsprout. None transcribed, translated, or published as written content. Blog is Lorem ipsum from 2018.

4

The "85 vs 100" proves no one reviews the site

Two contradicting experience claims on two live pages. No content QA process exists.

5

Their lead magnet is broken and they don't know

The eGuide download button does nothing. Every visitor who tries to download their lead magnet leaves empty-handed. Broken since migration.

6

They achieved Tier 1 Platinum despite all of this

Amazon EU's highest credential, Humble Group (40+ brands), SAS — all with a 2.5/10 digital presence. The business runs on relationships.

7

Expert-grade knowledge on intern-grade infrastructure

The defining characterization. Everything about their Amazon expertise is real. Everything about their digital infrastructure is broken. The gap is the largest we've seen in any prospect audit. This is solvable.
