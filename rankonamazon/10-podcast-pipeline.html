Executive Summary

Amazonpodden is the #1 Nordic Amazon podcast, hosted by Carl Helgesson since 2021. It contains 44 episodes and over 25 hours of expert Amazon content — covering product launches, PPC strategy, marketplace expansion, case studies, and industry interviews. All content is in Swedish.

None of this content exists in written form. There are no blog posts, no show notes on the website, no transcriptions, and no English translations. This is the single largest untapped content asset in their ecosystem.

Our pipeline converts this audio into 88-132 SEO-optimized English blog posts, social media clips, newsletter content, and lead magnets — all from content that already exists. The podcast becomes the engine that drives organic traffic, establishes English-language authority, and generates inbound leads across markets they currently cannot reach.

Pipeline Specification

Step 1: Audio Ingestion

Download podcast audio from Buzzsprout RSS feed

Input: RSS feed URL. Output: 44 MP3/M4A files. Automated via feed parser.

Step 2: Transcription

Whisper (large-v3) transcription — Swedish audio to Swedish text

Speaker diarization (pyannote) to separate Carl from guests. Timestamps for clip extraction. Processing: ~5 min per hour of audio on M1 Max.

Step 3: Translation

AI translation — Swedish to English with Amazon domain knowledge

Uses Claude for context-aware translation (not literal). Preserves Amazon terminology, brand names, and technical concepts. Maintains conversational tone where appropriate.

Step 4: SEO Optimization

Keyword research + content structuring for search ranking

Target keyword identification per post. H1/H2/H3 structure with keyword placement. Meta description, OG tags, Schema.org Article markup. Internal linking strategy across the content hub.

Step 5: Content Generation

Transform transcript into 2-3 polished blog posts per episode

Primary post: comprehensive article covering the episode topic (2,000-5,000 words). Secondary posts: listicles, how-to guides, or opinion pieces derived from subtopics. Each post has unique angle, CTA, and target keyword.

Step 6: Quality Review

Human review for accuracy, tone, and brand alignment

Carl or team reviews before publish. Focus: factual accuracy, brand voice, Amazon terminology correctness. We handle revisions based on feedback.

Step 7: Multi-Format Output

Publish and distribute across channels

Blog post (WordPress). Social media clips (LinkedIn, Twitter). Newsletter excerpt. Show notes page on website. Optional: YouTube clips with audiogram visualization.
