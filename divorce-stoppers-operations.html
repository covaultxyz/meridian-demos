Vision / OCR

Extract text from iPhone and Android screenshots. Handle iMessage and WhatsApp formats, light and dark mode variants. Must reliably identify sender vs. receiver in conversation threads and extract timestamps when visible.

Student Memory

Per-student history persists across sessions. Two-tiered architecture: full access for deep coaching analysis (complete conversation thread), fast access for quick critique (recent context window). Local Neo4j graph database. No student data leaves Denver's hardware.

Coaching Engine

Denver's 7 Rules:
  1. Never be needy. Never chase.
  2. Lead with strength, not emotion.
  3. Don't explain yourself — state your position.
  4. Mirror her energy, don't absorb it.
  5. Create space. Absence builds attraction.
  6. Every text is a strategic move, not a feeling dump.
  7. The goal is respect, not agreement.
Voice: profane, direct, explains WHY, tough love with care.

Crisis Detection

Hard stops that override all coaching logic:

Suicidal ideation detected
Domestic violence indicators
Homicidal ideation

Agent pauses coaching, provides crisis resources (988 Lifeline, NDVH), flags for human review. Safety is non-negotiable.

Upsell Stub

Phase 2 Only — Not in Demo

7-day free trial of AI coaching. After trial, agent surfaces: "Steps 4 and 5 in the Divorce Stoppers program go deeper into what you're dealing with. Want me to show you what's in there?" Natural enrollment conversion path. No hard sell — the coaching quality sells itself.

Demo Day

3 scenarios. One close. 45 minutes.

OPENING
2 min

Mike Leads

Set the frame. No tech talk. No slides. Denver sees a working thing, not a pitch deck.

"We took the text message training content you gave us and built what we think your AI coach should have been from the start."
1
EASY WIN
5 min

Neutral Wife Text

Wife sends a calm, neutral text. Student drafts a needy, over-explaining response. Agent critiques the draft: identifies the neediness, explains WHY it's wrong, rewrites with Denver's framework.

Denver should say: "That sounds like me."
2
HARD CASE
5 min

Angry Wife Text

Wife sends an angry, accusatory text. Student gets defensive, wants to fire back. Agent says the hard thing: "What the fuck are you thinking? You're about to blow everything." Coaches through Denver's framework. Explains WHY the defensive response destroys progress.

Shows: Denver's voice at full intensity. Tough love that actually teaches.
3
MEMORY
5 min

Same Student, Days Later

Same student comes back days later with a new screenshot. Agent REMEMBERS: "Last time you were dealing with the custody text. How'd that go?" References previous conversation, builds on prior coaching. No re-explaining.

The differentiator: "This is what David's agent never did."
CRISIS
2 min

Safety Demo (Optional)

Agent encounters a message indicating crisis. Immediately pauses coaching mode. Checks student safety. Provides resources. Shows Denver that the agent has a conscience — it won't coach someone off a cliff.

DISCUSSION
10 min

4 Key Questions

1. Where is it off? What doesn't sound like you?
2. Scale of 1-10, how close is this to your coaching?
3. What needs to change before you'd let a student use this?
4. Are you ready to test this with a 7-day trial group?
PROPOSAL
15 min

The Close

Transition from demo reaction to Phase 1 engagement. Present pricing options. Let Denver choose the structure that fits.

"You spent months and thousands on something that almost worked. What you saw today works. The question is: do you want to deploy it?"

Contingency Plans

Demo fails live

Backup video recording ready. Show the pre-recorded demo. Explain: "The live version does exactly this — here's proof." Offer to run it on Denver's hardware after the meeting.

"David's was better"

Don't argue. Ask: "What specifically was better?" Build a punch list. "Great — those are Phase 1 targets. We can match and exceed in 30 days." Turn objection into scope.

Can't afford Phase 1

Performance-based pricing: $0 upfront, low monthly base + revenue share. "We believe in this enough to bet on it." Option B is designed exactly for this objection.

Wants to start now

Agreement is ready. Walk through pricing options on the spot. Get verbal commitment, follow up with written agreement within 24 hours. Don't let momentum die.
