What the Extractions Provide

Three frameworks that define the content generation engine:

1. Brunson 12-Step Sales Page Flow (imp 9)

Search: mig_search("12-step sales page flow brunson")

The structural template for any sales page, landing page, or VSL:

1.  Headline (benefit hook)
2.  Sub-headline (supports headline)
3.  Opener (opens curiosity loop — "I'll reveal X by the end")
4.  Credibility (who you are, why listen)
5.  Story of discovery (how you found the solution)
6.  Product introduction
7.  Bullets (mystery: "3 ways to X without Y" + informational)
8.  Testimonials (what OTHERS say, not what you say)
9.  Price presentation (anchor high, show discount)
10. Value stacking (bonuses to increase perceived value)
11. Guarantee / risk reversal
12. Call to action

2. Schwartz Messaging Strategy (imp 9)

Search: mig_search("schwartz awareness levels") + mig_search("schwartz mass desire tools")

Awareness-level messaging — different copy per prospect awareness:

Level Headline Strategy Body Strategy
Most Aware Price/deal/urgency "Last chance: 40% off ends tonight"
Product Aware Proof/comparison "Here's why [product] beats [competitor]"
Solution Aware Category education "The 3 types of [solution] and which is right for you"
Problem Aware Symptom matching "Why you feel [symptom] and what to do about it"
Unaware Identity-based "If you value [identity trait], this is for you"

Desire research tools (use before writing any copy):

  1. Desire Ladder: "I want X" → "so I can Y" → "so I can Z" — peel to emotional core
  2. Three Layers of Truth (Fishman): What they'll TELL you / WON'T tell you / CAN'T tell you
  3. Three Dimensions: Score each desire on Urgency × Staying Power × Scope. Only pursue high-all-three.

3. Phillips ABCDOs + Storyworthy (imp 9 + imp 7)

Search: mig_search("ABCDOs storytelling phillips") + mig_search("story change over time")

Story injection for pitch coaching:
- Every pitch must contain a CHANGE moment (Storyworthy)
- Counters beat specs (ABCDOs-C) — when prospect objects, counter with story
- Duo technique (ABCDOs-D) — get prospect to tell their OWN story (releases oxytocin)
- Objects (ABCDOs-O) — physical/visual elements that trigger story conversations

How to Verify

Generate a landing page for a test product (e.g., Agent Vox itself):

  1. Does it follow all 12 Brunson steps in order?
  2. Does the opener create a curiosity loop?
  3. Does the story of discovery contain a CHANGE moment (Storyworthy)?
  4. Are bullets mystery-style ("3 ways to X without Y")?
  5. Is the price anchored before discount?
  6. Does the guarantee reverse risk?

Adapt for "problem-aware" audience:
7. Does the headline address a symptom, not a product?
8. Is there education before the product introduction?

Generate a 7-step email for niche audience:
9. Does step 3 include a plain YouTube link (not embedded)?
10. Do steps 4 and 6 contain lead magnets?
11. Is step 7 a soft guilt close?

Score each 0-10. Target: 7+ across all dimensions.
