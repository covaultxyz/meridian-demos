Phase 3 Knowledge Extraction Marathon (Final Report)

Session Arc

Three phases: (1) breadth sprint → (2) self-audit and depth correction → (3) crown jewel deep reads.

Phase 1 produced 29 shallow memories at high velocity. Lens/Prism audit caught the pattern:
2-13% transcript coverage producing table-of-contents-level indexing. The best insight
(static-fact bias) came from the one extraction that got the depth pressure pass.

Phase 2 corrected 6 shallow extractions with full-read replacements. Each replacement
surfaced operational tools the shallow pass missed entirely (e.g., Phillips ABCDOs: had
2 of 5 letters; Traffic Secrets: had ingredient list but not the 12-step sequence).

Phase 3 launched parallel agent deep reads on the Cleveland crown jewels (18,576 lines
across 3 files). Combined with a direct deep read of SOT Criteria Elicitation (1,120 lines),
produced three importance-10 memories that form the complete Agent Vox conversation engine.

Key Lesson

5 deep extractions beat 29 shallow ones. The three imp-10 memories could not have been
produced without full reads of the source transcripts. Cross-domain synthesis only emerges
from depth — you cannot synthesize what you have not witnessed.
