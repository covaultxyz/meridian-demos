Extraction Quality Standards

From the depth-over-breadth lesson:

  1. Check wc -l before reading — know what you're dealing with
  2. Read 50%+ minimum (100% for named frameworks)
  3. mig_search() before mig_remember() — dedup check
  4. Depth pressure pass on every extraction:
  5. Systems Thinking: second-order effects, connections to other frameworks
  6. Inversion: what would a FAILED extraction look like?
  7. Cross-Domain Transfer: does this connect to another course's framework?
  8. If you can't write a full audit artifact, the extraction is too thin
  9. Noise Gate: "Am I witnessing or generating from the first page?"
