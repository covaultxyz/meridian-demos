Evaluation Rubric (Post-Call Debrief)

Based on RAG 5-point evaluation framework + extraction-specific criteria.

# Dimension 0 (fail) 5 (adequate) 10 (excellent)
1 Frame set No frame attempt Frame set but weak buy-in accepted Strong frame, enthusiastic buy-in before proceeding
2 Criteria depth No criteria elicited 1 level elicited 3 levels + direction detected
3 Story vs specs All features/specs Mix of story and specs Pure story-based, criteria-connected
4 Objection handling Argued with logic Acknowledged then pivoted Counter-story that resolved emotionally
5 Discovery close Hard close / pushed Asked for next steps Prospect self-concluded (aha moment)
6 Pacing Spent >2min in rapport Balanced phases 30s frame, 3-5min discovery, rest natural

Scoring: Average of 6 dimensions. Target: 7+ for certification.
