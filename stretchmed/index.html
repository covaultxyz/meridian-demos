02

The Intelligence Layer

What We Will Build

A system that listens, remembers, and acts — built phase by phase, validated at each stage.

MERIDIAN will replace the gap between your fragmented tools with a persistent intelligence system that connects every call, every customer, every KPI, and every SOP — surfacing the patterns that drive revenue. Each capability below is deployed in the phase indicated, not all at once.

Voice AI & Call Recording — Phase 1

AI agents will handle inbound lead follow-up in English and Spanish, deployed in Phase 1 starting with a 3–5 location pilot. Every call recorded, transcribed, and scored against your sales script. Projected SDR cost reduction: up to $33K/month (dependent on AI call quality matching human parity). State-by-state consent framework designed and pending legal validation before any recording goes live.

Top Performer Cloning — Phase 2

Once Phase 1 generates sufficient call recordings, AI will analyze your best closers — language, pacing, objection handling, question sequence — and generate a training playbook from actual data. AI practice prospects at graded difficulty levels will be built for new hire training.

Persistent Intelligence System — Phase 2

A connected intelligence system will be built to store customer interactions, SOPs, KPIs, and insights — all searchable in plain English. Ask it anything: "What's the close rate in the Southeast?" and get an answer with the source. Available once data migration from Ments and other sources is complete. Requires: Ments API access or data export capability — a Phase 1 prerequisite to confirm.

KPI Command Center — Phase 2

A real-time dashboard will be built, accessible via Telegram, with leadership and analyst views. AI-generated alerts (e.g., "Brooklyn's close rate dropped 12% this week") will be enabled once connected to live data feeds from Ments and call recordings.

Retention Intelligence — Phase 3

A churn prediction model will be trained on your member data (visit frequency, engagement patterns, session feedback) once data access is established. Automated re-engagement sequences and progress reports with ROM improvements will be deployed to extend retention toward the 18-month industry benchmark.

Franchise Sales Enrichment — Phase 3

An enrichment pipeline will be built to instantly enhance inbound franchise leads with AI-generated personalized outreach and pre-call prospect simulation. At $250K+ LTV per franchise sale, even a modest improvement in conversion velocity can offset the full engagement cost.

Phase 1 is designed to answer this question with data, not promises. If Voice AI doesn't match SDR conversion rates, if call recording hits legal blockers, or if the sales scorecard doesn't surface actionable patterns — you find out in 30 days at $4,500, not in 6 months at $89,500.

Every phase has a go/no-go gate. If a phase doesn't meet its success criteria, we diagnose the problem and decide together whether to fix it, adjust scope, or stop. There is no lock-in beyond the current phase.
